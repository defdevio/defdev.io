About

Anthony Owens, the founder of DefDev, possesses nearly twenty years of total experience in the technology sector. He has supported various businesses, ranging from small MSPs to large hotel chains and major financial institutions like RBC, in effectively integrating cloud technology.

Recognizing an opportunity to provide emerging industries with the necessary tools to navigate the evolving technological landscape, Anthony established DefDev with a focus on exceptional tech mentorship.

Anthony is dedicated to nurturing talent within the United States to develop the skills necessary for local communities, and organizations alike, to thrive amid the evolving challenges of the modern digital age.

Anthony and his team are ready to support your digital transformation through training or implementation, no matter the scale. DefDev can help!
